Follow me

I am a person who is used to living in different countries, even though I did not even think about it. Because of the war, I had to move from Ukraine to Spain. And I am glad to be in this country and with such kind people!

I love nature and beautiful places, especially in the presence of a decent society.

I say "decent society" because I see some people throwing garbage on the ground, leaving a bad impression, and also behaving inappropriately.

I wish that wars such as in Ukraine, Azerbaijan, etc., and the injustices on this earth are over!

But in general, I can say that our world is beautiful, and in the presence of kind and decent people, our world becomes even more beautiful.
